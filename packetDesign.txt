Each packet will be in 3 bytes

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|  			    1				    |  							2							|							3					 |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|			Start Byte		    | [D]irection & [M]otor Byte  |				PWM Val					 |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|					0xBE					|						XXXX XXDM					|	 1111 1111 - 0000 0000 |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


if D = 0 , direction = forward
if D = 1 , direction = backward

if M = 0 , motor = motor_one
if M = 1 , motor = motor_two				


Alernative:
Each packet will be 4 bytes

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|  			    1				    |  							2							|					 	3	 	  			 |				 		4	  				 |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|			Start Byte		    |    [R]/[L] Direction Byte   |				 PWM Val L			 |				PWM Val R				 |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
|					0xBE					|						XXXX XXRL					|	 1111 1111 - 0000 0000 |	 1111 1111 - 0000 0000 |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


if L = 0 , left motor direction = forward
if L = 1 , left direction = backward

if R = 0 , right motor direction = forward
if R = 1 , right direction = backward
